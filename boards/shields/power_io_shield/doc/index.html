ARDEP Power IO Shield¶

Contents:

  • Power IO Inputs
  • ARDEP Interface
  • Power IO Outputs
  • Power IO Voltage Supply

The Power IO Shield extends the ARDEP platform’s power capabilities. It is intended for switching high-power loads and being triggered by 12V+ signal levels.

The shield offers six 48V-capable inputs and 6 high-side output drivers capable of driving 3A+ each and a total current of 10A for supply voltages of up to 48V.
